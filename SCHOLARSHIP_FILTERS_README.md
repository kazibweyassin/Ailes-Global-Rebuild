## 🚀 How to Use

### Step 1: Click "Filters" Button
Go to the scholarships page and click the "Filters" button to open the filter panel.

### Step 2: Select Your Criteria
Choose the criteria that match your situation:
- What degree are you pursuing?
- What's your field of study?
- How much funding do you need?
- When can you apply?
- What does the scholarship need to cover?

### Step 3: Watch Results Update
As you apply filters, the scholarship list updates automatically. You'll see:
- Number of matching scholarships
- Active filters displayed as tags
- Relevant scholarships listed below

### Step 4: Take Action
- **Browse**: Scroll through filtered results
- **Save**: Click the heart icon to save favorites  
- **Compare**: Use CSV export to compare scholarships
- **Share**: Share your filtered search with friends
- **Apply**: Click to go to scholarship website

## ❓ FAQ

**Q: Do I have to use all the filters?**
A: No! Use as few or as many as you need. Even 1-2 filters significantly narrow down results.

**Q: Can I combine different types of filters?**
A: Absolutely! All filters work together. The more filters, the more targeted your results.

**Q: What if I want to see all scholarships again?**
A: Click "Clear All" button to reset all filters and see all 861 scholarships.

**Q: Can I save my filter preferences?**
A: Currently filters reset on page reload, but you can use the Share button to get a URL you can save.

**Q: Do these filters work on mobile?**
A: Yes! Filters are fully functional and optimized for mobile devices.

**Q: Is there a cost to use these filters?**
A: No! Filters are included free with AILES Global.
