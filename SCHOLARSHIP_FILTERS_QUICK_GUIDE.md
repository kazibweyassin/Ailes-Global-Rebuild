## Filter Types Explained

### ✅ AND Logic (Combined)
Multiple filters = **ALL conditions must match**
```
Example:
  Field: Engineering AND
  Degree: Master's AND
  Amount: >$20K
  = Scholarships matching ALL three
```

### ✅ OR Logic (Within Field)
Multiple selections in same category
```
Example:
  Field: (Engineering OR Computer Science)
  = Scholarships for either field
```
